type in motion

"For the previous project, we worked on creating a flexible identity system. We looked at how we could create a brand that evolves depending on its context within a space — how does it change and move? How does its context effect how it appears? For our final project, we will build upon your system and look at how your identity system moves through not just space, but also time. You will create a 15-second to 1-minute animation for your brand. This could either be an animation showcasing the brand system (Whitney, MIT, etc), a short commercial or advertisement, (Knoll, Prada, etc), or even a film bumper or trailer. Think about what would be appropriate for your brand and where this animation might live (Instagram post vs. movie theater screen). The animation should have sound — whether a musical soundtrack or a series of sound effects. You may incorporate found or stock video footage in addition to your own animation if needed, but the logo itself should have some sort animation or movement added to it."
